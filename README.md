# BrandKarlo — Digital Marketing Agency Website

BrandKarlo is a multi-page marketing website for a digital marketing agency based in India. The site showcases the agency's services, past projects, and provides a way for potential clients to get in touch.

**Live Site:** [https://brandkarlo.vercel.app/](https://brandkarlo.vercel.app/)

## 🚀 About

BrandKarlo helps startups, local businesses, and growing brands achieve measurable growth through SEO, Google Ads, Social Media Marketing, Website Development, Content Marketing, and AI-powered digital strategies.

## 📄 Pages

| Page | File | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| Home | `index.html` | Hero section, agency overview, services summary, featured projects, and contact form |
| About | `about.html` | Detailed information about the agency and its mission |
| Services | `service.html` | Full breakdown of all services offered |
| Projects | `project.html` | Portfolio of completed client work |
| Contact | `contact.html` | Contact form and business details |

## 🛠️ Services Offered

- **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** — Improve Google rankings and drive organic traffic
- **Website Design & Development** — Fast, responsive, conversion-focused websites
- **Social Media Marketing** — Audience growth on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and more
- **AI Marketing Automation** — Automated customer engagement and lead nurturing
- **Google Ads (PPC)** — High-performing paid ad campaigns
- **Branding & Graphic Design** — Logos, creatives, and brand identity assets

## 📬 Contact Section

The site includes a contact form (Name, Email, Subject, Message) along with business contact details in the footer.

## 🎨 Sections on Homepage

1. Hero — headline, CTA buttons ("Get Free Consultation", "View Services")

6. Footer — company info, quick links, project gallery thumbnails, social links
